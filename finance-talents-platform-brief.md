# Finance Talents — Platform Brief

## Slogan
> **Elite finance jobs. One platform.**

---

## What We Do

Finance Talents is the go-to destination for high-end finance jobs and internships. We bring together the best opportunities in Private Equity, Venture Capital, FinTech, and Investment Banking — all in one place.

We connect ambitious professionals at every level with the firms that are shaping the future of finance: from boutique investment firms to global corporates and fast-growing scale-ups.

---

## Mission

To make elite finance careers more accessible by centralising the best opportunities in one focused, curated platform.

---

## Target Audience

### Candidates
- Finance professionals at all seniority levels (analyst to MD)
- Ambitious students and graduates seeking internships
- Professionals looking to move into PE, VC, IB or FinTech

### Companies
- Boutique investment firms
- Scale-ups in FinTech and VC-backed businesses
- Global corporates with finance divisions

---

## Core Categories

- **Private Equity** — Buy-side roles across fund sizes and strategies
- **Venture Capital** — From scout to partner, early and growth stage
- **Investment Banking** — M&A, ECM, DCM, coverage and advisory
- **FinTech** — Finance roles at technology-driven financial companies

---

## Platform Features

- Job listings with company context
- Company profiles — who they are, what they invest in, culture
- Filter by seniority, category, location, and contract type
- International focus, English as primary language

---

## Tone of Voice

**Modern. Direct. Premium.**

Finance Talents speaks like a trusted insider — someone who knows the industry and respects the ambition of the people using the platform. We don't use jargon for the sake of it, but we don't oversimplify either.

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Clear and confident | Stiff or corporate |
| Ambitious and forward-looking | Hype or buzzwords |
| Warm but professional | Casual or informal |
| Concise — every word earns its place | Long-winded explanations |

### Voice Examples

❌ "We offer a comprehensive suite of career solutions for finance professionals."
✅ "Elite finance jobs. One platform."

❌ "Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology to optimise your job search."
✅ "Find your next role in PE, VC, IB or FinTech — without the noise."

## Visual Identity

### Colours
- **Background:** Light grey `#EBEBEB`
- **Primary text:** Dark anthracite `#222222`
- **Accent:** Red `#D93025`

### Direction
Clean layouts, strong typography, generous whitespace. Editorial, not corporate. Authority without arrogance.

- **Whitespace** — let content breathe
- **Typography** — sharp, modern, high contrast
- **Imagery** — offices, cities, people — aspirational but real
- **Logo style** — lowercase, bracketed `(finance talents)` — approachable yet distinctive

## Positioning

> "Finance Talents is where the best finance professionals find their next move — and where the best firms find their next hire."

We are not a generalist job board. We are a curated platform built specifically for the finance industry. Every listing, every company profile, and every piece of content is relevant to our audience.

---

## Language

- **Primary language:** English
- **Tone:** International, professional, inclusive of all experience levels
- **Avoid:** Dutch-only references, overly local framing
